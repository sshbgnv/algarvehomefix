FAQ

Common questions

How much does AC servicing cost in Algarve?

Standard service costs €90–140 per unit and includes filter and coil cleaning, refrigerant check and a performance test. Regas starts from €120. We always give a free quote before any work begins.

Which areas of Algarve do you cover?

The whole Algarve — from Sagres to Olhão. Including Vilamoura, Quarteira, Faro, Albufeira, Almancil, Loulé, Vale do Lobo, Quinta do Lago, Tavira, Lagos and Portimão.

How quickly can you respond?

We aim to respond within 24 hours and often arrange a same-day or next-day visit. Fastest way: WhatsApp — send a photo of the problem and we'll reply straight away.

Are you insured?

Yes, fully insured for all work carried out at your property.

Do you work in English?

Yes. We work in English and Russian — which is why many expat homeowners and holiday rental managers across Algarve choose us.

Can you handle multiple jobs in one visit?

Yes, and it's often the most efficient option. Many clients combine AC servicing with minor repairs, TV or curtain rail installation, or a pre-checkin inspection — all in one trip.

Do you prepare Airbnb and holiday rental properties?

Yes. We carry out pre-checkin inspections, urgent repairs between bookings, and full season preparation before the summer rental period. See our Rental Preparation page for details.

Is the quote really free?

Yes — always. Send us a photo on WhatsApp, describe what you need, and we'll come back with a clear price. No commitment, no call-out fee.
