🔧 AC Servicing, Cleaning & Regas

This is the most requested job, and the one that prevents the most trouble later. A unit that hasn't been serviced loses cooling power and uses more electricity. We clean the filters and coils, check the refrigerant level, inspect the electrics and confirm the system is running at full performance — ideally booked before the Algarve summer heat arrives.

Filter & coil cleaning
Refrigerant check & regas
Electrical inspection
Performance test
