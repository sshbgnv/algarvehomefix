Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This privacy policy explains how Algarve Home Fix ("we", "us") collects and uses personal data when you contact us through this website, by phone, or by email. We are committed to handling your data in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and applicable Portuguese data protection law.

1. Who we are

Algarve Home Fix is a home maintenance and property preparation service operating across the Algarve region of Portugal.

Contact: Algarvehomefix@gmail.com  ·  +351 913 981 175

2. What data we collect

We collect only the information you provide voluntarily when you contact us:

  • Your name
  • Phone number or email address
  • A description of the work you need done and your property location

If you contact us via WhatsApp, your message and phone number are received through WhatsApp's platform (governed by their own privacy policy). If you contact us by email, your message and email address are stored in our inbox. We do not operate a contact form that stores submissions in a database.

Our web hosting provider may collect standard server logs (IP address, page visited, timestamp) as part of normal operation. We do not access or process these logs for marketing or profiling purposes.

3. How we use your data

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Respond to your enquiry or quote request
  • Schedule and confirm service appointments
  • Follow up on completed work if needed

We do not use your data for marketing, profiling, or automated decision-making of any kind.

4. Who we share data with

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for commercial purposes. Your contact details remain with us and are used only for the purpose for which they were provided. In cases where a sub-contractor is needed to complete a job, any sharing of relevant information will only happen with your knowledge.

5. Cookies and tracking

This website uses Google Analytics 4 to collect anonymous visitor statistics — pages viewed, session duration, approximate geographic location (country/region level), and device type. This data is collected via cookies set by Google and is used solely to understand how visitors use the site so we can improve it. No personally identifiable information is included in these reports.

Google Analytics data is processed by Google LLC under their own privacy policy (policies.google.com/privacy). You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking across all websites by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Beyond Google Analytics, no advertising trackers or other third-party tracking scripts are used. Additional external resources loaded on this site — Google Fonts, jsDelivr CDN (icons), and Tailwind CSS — do not collect personal data about visitors on our behalf.

6. How long we keep your data

We retain contact details for as long as there is an active service relationship or ongoing enquiry. If no service has been booked within 12 months of initial contact, we delete the details from our records. You can also request deletion at any time (see section 8).

7. Your rights under GDPR

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Lodge a complaint with the Portuguese data protection authority (CNPD) at cnpd.pt

8. How to contact us about your data

For any data-related request — access, correction, or deletion — please email Algarvehomefix@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Request". We will respond within 30 days.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page reflects when it was last revised. Continued use of this website after a revision constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
